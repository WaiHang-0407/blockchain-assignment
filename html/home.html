Welcome to FundHub

Crowdfunding is a process that allows individuals or organizations to raise small amounts of capital from a large number of people to fund a project or venture.

Traditional crowdfunding platforms are centralized and often charge high fees, delay fund distribution, and lack transparency in how the funds are managed.

A decentralized crowdfunding platform can overcome these issues by using blockchain technology and smart contracts to ensure that funds are collected, stored, and distributed in a transparent and trustless manner.

Our dApp enables users to create, fund, and manage crowdfunding campaigns through Ethereum smart contracts. Project creators can launch campaigns, and contributors can fund them directly without intermediaries.

The system automatically handles goal achievements, refunds, and reward distributions through on-chain logic, ensuring fairness and transparency for everyone involved.
